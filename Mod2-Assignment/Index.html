Some bands, some album

Don Caballero

What burn never returns is basically the natural and most succesfull evolution of the progressive rock. 48 minutes of instrumental looped music, with insane drumming and astonishing melodies.

Flaming Lips

Clouds taste metallic, best band album from 1995. Insane songs, insange groove and insane lyrics. everything is insane, about this album! Best song, imho, is "This Here Giraffe".

Liars

Drum's not dead is the third album of this band. It's very creepy and suitable for a horro movie. It has an obscure sound, made entirley by drum samples and weird recording and editing.
